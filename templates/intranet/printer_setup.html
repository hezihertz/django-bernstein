Add Network Printers

We currently have three printer for the group: a HP Laserjet P4015 (B/W), a HP Color LaserJet CP4005 (Color) and a RICOH Aficio MP 2815 (B/W copier) . All are accessible through the JetDirect TCP-IP Network printing protocol. Here are the IP addresses for the three printers:

RICOH Aficio MP C300SR (color copier):128.103.96.206

HP Laserjet P4015 (Black and White):128.103.92.232

Follow the instructions on configuring wireless printer for Windows, Mac, and other operating systerms.

If you can't find the driver for the exact same printer model, try a similar one, there is a good chance it will work. And don't forget to setup "Double-Sided Printing" on all printers!

Always use RICOH Aficio MP C300SR (copier) if you can, it prints faster, and costs a lot less than the other.
